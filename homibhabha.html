And To promote friendly relations between nationalist political workers from different parts of the country They also want to improve infrastructure in india. And want to improve our education system more better for the future youth. HBP believes in equality in all religion and castes.
They promise that they will provide all facilities to all religions and castes people in all over india. party support voting rights and minority rights, students rights including LGBT rights HBP ruled for 10 years in maharashtra. And they have done some good works in their serving years. Like providing electricity in low cost, and gas cylinders also. they have created a solar power plants in maharashtra that produce electricity in some region of maharashtra. HBP is currently working on elections all over india. And they are doing some social activities too. HBP stands in election from all states of india.this party is also giving importance to agricultural field and science field also. to make india greener environmental and modern country as well.
